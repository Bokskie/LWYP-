AboutUs
Our basketball club has a rich history marked by both triumphs and challenges. The term "lawaya" is a significant aspect of our journey, embodying the resilience and determination we've cultivated over the years. Initially, it was a term associated with our relentless passion for the game, fueled by the countless hours we spent playing under the guidance of our mentor, Tatay William.

The word "lawaya" took on a dual meaning for us, reflecting not only the intensity of our commitment but also the struggles we faced, particularly in refining our skills in mobile legends (ML). It became apparent that achieving proficiency in ML required a passive approach, emphasizing strategy and teamwork over individual prowess. This realization prompted us to merge the concepts of "lawaya" and "passive," forging a balanced and effective playing style.

To elevate our game further, we embarked on a basketball bootcamp under the watchful eye of Tatay William. The proximity of the training facility allowed us to foster a deeper connection within our group, strengthening the bonds of camaraderie and unity. Our enduring commitment to one another has been a cornerstone of our success, as we've weathered challenges and celebrated victories together.

Our basketball journey has not been limited to our local courts; we have actively participated in various league tournaments, showcasing our skills and sportsmanship. The spirit of competition has fueled our desire to continually improve and represent our club with pride. As we look back on the milestones we've achieved, it's clear that our basketball club is more than just a team – it's a tightly-knit community driven by a shared love for the game and an unwavering commitment to success.

In essence, our club's story is one of resilience, growth, and the enduring power of unity. Through the highs and lows, the "lawaya" spirit has been our guiding force, propelling us forward on our basketball journey.
